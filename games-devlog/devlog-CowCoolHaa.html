Process of making : CowCoolHaa GaHenChop

about

"Cowcoolhaa GaHenChop" is the Dress Up Game that created as a parody to Thailand's election event. Player needs to gather items in the scene and attach it to your face to impersonate human and do the election.

Project Info

  • Role : Programmer
  • Team Size: 13
  • Time Period: 3 days
  • Tools: Unity Engine

In every year, I usually attended in on-site Global Game Jam. This year (2026) is the first year that I decided to attended online. Because on-site registration fulled in less than a week. I'm glad that there is a lot of people interested in attending the event, but I'm also hope that I've seen it sooner.

When the theme "Mask" is announced. We decided to make a game that focusing on disguise yourself through something. And out of nowhere, We want to parodied the current Thailand's election event. It was just for fun at that moment,

Some parts of the brainstorm session...

The main features in this game is to disguise by plug random things that player can found in front of election area on player's face. this part can made inside code by calculating the normals on the player's surface and rotate the object using that value.

The next part is to detect where the object is plugged in (ex. hair, nose, mouth, etc.) This part we use normal collision in each area to detect it.

And this is the part where I messed up. I responsible for merging all codes and needs to check the data transfer over scenes. in the last hour of jam, The data transter doesn't work so the appearance detail is not updated. at the end, the gameplay is working, but the data is still not transfer and the game force player to lose even though you have the win condition.
